About the Project

The Multimodal Bully Classifier is an advanced AI-driven project aimed at identifying harmful and bullying content. By using multimodal deep learning models, the system analyzes both images and text to detect bullying behaviors effectively.

Our goal is to contribute to a safer and more inclusive online environment by providing accurate and actionable insights.

  • Multimodal Analysis: Image and text processing
  • Deep Learning Architecture: VGG16 model , XLM-RoBERTa + BiGRU model
  • High Performance: 74% accuracy
